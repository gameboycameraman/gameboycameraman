Gameboycameraman's Camera

Hi and welcome to my Game Boy Camera inspired project.

I've created this free experiment for everyone and I hope a lot of people will have a chance to play with these pixels!

You can take a picture and then save it. Saving it will display the picture on this page, and you will then be able to save it on your phone or computer.

You will find a video tutorial on my instagram profile Gameboycameraman.
